zl187_TSH_test_data_conversion.diagnosis(TSH)¶

Diagnose the patients by the input TSH test results

“hyperthyroidism” - any of their tests results being less than 1.0, “hypothyroidism” - any of their test results being greater than 4.0, or “normal thyroid function” - all of their test results being

between 1.0 and 4.0, inclusive.

No single patient will have test results both above 4.0 and below 1.0, hence will only meet one of the diagnoses above.

Parameters:TSH – TSH results from patients
Returns:diagnoses of all patients from TSH results input
Return type:diag
